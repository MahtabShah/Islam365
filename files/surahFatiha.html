بِسۡمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحۡمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

Allah - beginning with the name of - the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful

ٱلۡحَمۡدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ ٱلۡعَٰلَمِينَ

[Fatihah 1:1] All praise is to Allah, the Lord Of The Creation.

ٱلرَّحۡمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

[Fatihah 1:2] The Most Gracious, the Most Merciful

مَٰلِكِ يَوۡمِ ٱلدِّينِ

[Fatihah 1:3] Owner of the Day of Recompense

إِيَّاكَ نَعۡبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسۡتَعِينُ

[Fatihah 1:4] You alone we worship and from You alone we seek help (and may we always).

ٱهۡدِنَا ٱلصِّرَٰطَ ٱلۡمُسۡتَقِيمَ

[Fatihah 1:5] Guide us on the Straight Path.

صِرَٰطَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَنۡعَمۡتَ عَلَيۡهِمۡ غَيۡرِ ٱلۡمَغۡضُوبِ عَلَيۡهِمۡ وَلَا ٱلضَّآلِّينَ

[Fatihah 1:6] The path of those whom You have favoured - Not the path of those who earned Your anger - nor of those who are astray.
(Amen - So be it)
